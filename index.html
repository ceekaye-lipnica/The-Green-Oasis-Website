WHAT WE DO

The Green Oasis was Introduced in early 2017 by Klassic Marketing Inc. The firm is a full service landscaping company that has been addressing the demands of residential and commercial clients. The company’s reputation is founded on its dedication in providing high-quality landscaping services. Creating various landscape designs to cater the preferences of the customers. To provide the right knowledge and expertise to ensure beauty, growth, and maintenance of the property. To transform customers’ vision design and provide them assurance by delivering outstanding service. Offer ideas and advice to enhance and beautify homeowners or business owner’s commercial or residential land.

What isThe Green Oasis?

Our Service guarantee is simple. We’re not happy until you’re happy.


We are Professional landscape and design specialists dedicated to providing landscape quality services; designing attractive spaces and maintaining their functionality and beauty for homeowners, businesses, schools, and other organizations in an affordable price range.

VISION

The Green Oasis’s vision is to be one of the leading landscape specialists in the country that offers quality landscape management services, cultivating outdoor designs, irrigation, detail-orientation, innovation and efficiency that meets customers’ expectations

MISSION

  • To build and sustain flourishing landscapes through the use of ecological garden designs and management practices.
  • To meet the functional demands of the customer with innovative solutions that are both practical and spectacular.
  • To constantly incorporate innovative and effective approaches for sustainable garden development and installation.
  • To ensure quality work where experts handle the fields and to guarantee you a realistic insight of your project.
  • To provide high quality landscaping, irrigation installations and maintenance to meet client’s expectations.
